Photo-based condition assessment

Upload photos of the vehicle from multiple angles — front, rear, both sides, close-ups of any existing damage. Up to 10 photos, max 8 MB each.

The AI checks image quality (sharpness, lighting, vehicle presence), estimates the capture angle, and produces a per-photo condition signal. Damage detection is a demo heuristic — swap for a trained model in production.
